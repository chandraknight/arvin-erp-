How unit conversion works: Each product has a Purchase Unit (how you buy it, e.g. KG, Pack of 12) and a Sale Unit (how you sell it, e.g. Gram, Piece). The Conversion Factor tells how many sale units are in one purchase unit (e.g. 1 KG = 1000 g, 1 Pack = 12 pcs). Stock is always stored and sold in sale units. When a Purchase Order is received, the quantity is automatically multiplied by the conversion factor.
