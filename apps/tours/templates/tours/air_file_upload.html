About AIR Files

An Agency Invoice Report (AIR) is the BSP billing summary that shows what your agency owes IATA for a billing period — total sales, refunds, commission earned, taxes, and the net amount due.

Supported formats:

  • IATA fixed-width text (.air, .txt) — amounts are parsed automatically
  • CSV summary export (.csv) — flexible column names
  • Excel (.xlsx / .xls) — first sheet used
